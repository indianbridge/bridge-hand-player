Play out the hand Tool Instructions

  1. This tool lets you play out bridge hands in double dummy fashion to experiment the outcome of different lines of play.
  2. Hands are specified using the format of BridgeBase Hand Viewer. Instructions on that format can be found at http://www.bridgebase.com/tools/hvdoc.html. While the hand viewer supports several parameters listed at the end of the above link, only a subset of them can be used with this tool. The supported parameters are
    1. a auction
    2. b board number
    3. d dealer
    4. e East hand
    5. n North
    6. s South
    7. v vulernability
    8. w West
  3. After a hand has loaded you can click on cards to play ( cards that can be played are highlighted with a green outline ). The undo and redo buttons can be used to take back and redo plays.
